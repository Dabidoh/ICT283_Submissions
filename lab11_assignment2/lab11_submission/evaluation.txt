For data structures, I used a template BST and std::map. The BST is used to store unique dates and demonstrate the required tree structure from Lab 10. Full weather observations are stored in WeatherLog, while unique dates are stored separately in the BST. I chose this design because it keeps main.cpp shorter and moves the application logic into WeatherLog. This is an improvement over my earlier Lab 8 design, where too much logic was placed directly in main.
